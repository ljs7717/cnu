    About the Sponsors

    Korea Foundation

     

    The purposes of the Korea Foundation are to promote proper awareness and understanding of Korea, and to enhance goodwill and friendship throughout the international community through a diverse array of international exchange activities, in pursuant to Article 1 of the Korea Foundation Act.

    One Korea Foundation

     

    Hope Is Where the Heart Is The One Korea Foundation, a non-profit corporation operating in Washington, D.C. area, holds peaceful unification of Korea with freedom, democracy, rule of law and human rights for all as the paramount goal. The Foundation conducts educational and advocacy activities (e.g., seminars, lectures, debates, information campaigns, public testimonials, and cultural events) to encourage the public and policy makers toward accepting the doctrine of Hongik Ingan (홍익인간 /弘益人間: Attaining broad benefit for Humankind) as the governing motto of united Korea and the Declaration for One Korea as the guiding principle of efforts for unification. OKF actively seeks opportunities for joint endeavors in partnership or coalition with other individuals and groups with similar objectives

    Institute for Peace Affairs

     

    The Institute for Peace Affairs was established as a nonprofit and nonpolitical organization pursuant to rational point of view in approaching to North Korea and unification affairs in 1983. The Primary goal of IPA is to develop a perspective of Korean unification through various researches and public information activities based on materializing of North Korea and unification affairs. To promote this objective and contribute to peace in the world and Northeast Asia, IPA has pushed for enhancing exchange and cooperation activities with institutes concerned, both domestic and international.
